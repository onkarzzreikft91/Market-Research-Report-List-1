<p><p>Rolling road markets can be categorized into two main types: single roller type and multi roller type. The single roller type features a single rolling mechanism that allows for a simpler setup and ease of use, making it suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, the multi roller type utilizes multiple rollers to enable simultaneous testing or more complex operations, providing greater efficiency and flexibility. Both types serve specific needs in evaluating vehicle performance and tire characteristics under controlled conditions.</p></p>
