<p><p>EVOH films for packaging are designed to provide excellent barrier properties, making them ideal for preserving food and pharmaceutical products. The market can be divided into two main types: blown films and cast films. Blown films are produced through an air-inflated process, resulting in higher strength and thickness, suitable for commercial applications. In contrast, cast films are manufactured by casting molten polymer onto a surface, offering superior clarity and flexibility, which are often preferred for retail packaging. Both types enhance product shelf life and quality.</p></p>
