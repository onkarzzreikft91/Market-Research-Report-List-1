<p><p>The PCR workstation market encompasses various types tailored to specific applications. Multiplex PCR allows simultaneous amplification of multiple targets, enhancing efficiency. Single-cell PCR focuses on analyzing individual cells, crucial for precision in research. Long-range PCR extends the amplification of larger DNA fragments, vital in genomic studies. Methylation-specific PCR (MSP) targets methylated DNA, important for epigenetic research. Fast-cycling PCR accelerates the amplification process, improving throughput. Collectively, these technologies cater to diverse research and diagnostic needs in molecular biology.</p></p>
